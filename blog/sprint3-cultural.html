Kahu Leary

"What is emotional intelligence? "

Emotional intelligence or EQ (Emotional Quotient) is the ability to understand and manage your own emotions, as well as the those of the people around you in a healthy and conscientious way. People with a high degree of emotional intelligence know what they're feeling, what their emotions mean, and how these emotions can affect the people around them.

"Why is emotional intelligence important?"

Emotional intelligence is an important skill that is benifical in all aspects of life, it allows you to have better relationships with the people around you such as peers/co-workers, friends and family, and even yourself. Research has found that people with a high EQ perform better and achieve more in their careers as well as other aspects of their lives, and is already recognised by employers as a very important factor to consider when hiring employees.

"The difference between IQ and EQ"

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a score of ones intellectual ability based off several tests and is used to measure and assess ones academic ability and intelligence.

EQ (Emotional Quotient) is an indviduals ability to understand and be aware of ones emotions and feelings as well as those of others and to manage them in a healthy and thoughtful way.
